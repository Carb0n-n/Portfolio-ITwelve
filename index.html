About Valve

VALVe Corporation, also known as VALVe Software, is an American video game developer, publisher, and digital distribution company headquartered in Bellevue, Washington. It is the developer of the software distribution platform Steam and the game franchises Half-Life, Counter-Strike, Portal, Day of Defeat, Team Fortress 2, Left 4 Dead and Dota.
